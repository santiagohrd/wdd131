About Me

Hi! My name is Santiago Herrera, and I’m passionate about animals and nature. This site reflects my love for learning about fascinating creatures and sharing their unique traits. My goal is to inspire others to care for and protect these amazing beings.

About This Project

This project is designed to educate and spread awareness about some of my favorite animals for WDD131 course. From interesting facts to conservation efforts, the content here aims to highlight the beauty, intelligence, and importance of animals in our world.
